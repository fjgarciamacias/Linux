# Standard

## Basic

* `cd [dir]`: *Change Directory*

* `ls [dir | file]`: *List* files 

* `pwd`: *Print Working Directory*



## Busqueda

* `grep`: *Global Regular Expressions Print*
  * `grep -E` == `egrep`
* `egrep`: *Extended Global Regular Expressions Print*
  * `egrep -v '^#|^$'`: Delete comments (#) and new lines
